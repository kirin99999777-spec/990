Spinach, Tofu and Sesame Stir-Fry

A healthy, flavorful weeknight meal

By: Martha Rose Shulman
Yield: 3 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ pound tofu, cut in small dice
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated or minced fresh ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 16-ounce bag baby spinach, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Preparation

Step 1: Heat the canola oil over medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet or wok, and add the tofu. Stir-fry until lightly colored, three to five minutes, and add the garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. Cook until fragrant, about one minute, and add soy sauce to taste. Add the spinach and stir-fry until wilted, about one minute. Stir in the sesame seeds, and add more soy sauce to taste. Remove from heat.

Step 2: Using tongs, transfer the mixture to a serving bowl, leaving liquid behind in the pan. Drizzle with sesame oil and add more soy sauce as desired. Serve with rice, grains, or noodles. It can also be used as a filling for pita bread.

Tip: This is a last-minute dish, but you can prepare all ingredients in advance.
